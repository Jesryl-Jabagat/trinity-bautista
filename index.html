About Us

We offer a variety of traditional and modern Filipino snacks to satisfy your cravings during your merienda time. Our mission is to bring the taste of the Philippines to you, wherever you are.

Our Snacks

Check out our delicious Filipino snacks, perfect for any merienda break.

Halo-halo

A refreshing Filipino dessert with shaved ice, milk, and mixed fruits.

Turon

Deep-fried banana spring rolls with caramelized sugar coating.

Taho

Soft tofu dessert with sweet syrup and tapioca pearls.

Bibingka

A soft rice cake traditionally cooked in a clay pot with banana leaves.

Mais con Yelo

A refreshing dessert with sweet corn, crushed ice, and milk.

French Fries

Classic snacks of fried potato sticks, crispy and gold.

Scramble

Shaved ice with a vibrant pink color, flavored with milk and syrup.

Puto Cheese

Soft steamed rice cake topped with melted cheese.
